Create an order

  • Uri: orders
  • Method: POST
  • Note: you will get in the response the "location" of the new your order.
  • Example of HEADERS: 
    Content-Type: application/vnd.restbucks+xml
  • Example CONTENT 
    <order xmlns:xsd="http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema" xmlns:xsi="http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema-instance" xmlns="http://restbuckson.net">
    <location>inShop</location>
    <items>
    <item>
        <name>latte</name>
        <quantity>1</quantity>
        <size>large</size>
        <milk>skim</milk>
    </item>
    </items>
</order>
